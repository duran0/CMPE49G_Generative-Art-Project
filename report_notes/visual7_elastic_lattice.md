# Elastic Lattice

Story:
This image starts from a rigid grid and then subjects it to conflicting pressures. The result suggests an engineered surface that has been bent by invisible fields rather than broken outright.

Method:
Horizontal and vertical lines are sampled densely and warped by a combination of attractor and repulsor fields, low-frequency curl, and Perlin-noise displacement. Plotting both families of lines preserves the sense of a stretched fabric.

Parameters:
- Seed: 42
- Grid lines: 27 horizontal and 27 vertical
- Points per line: 180
- Noise scale: 1.85
- Noise displacement: 0.035
- Field strengths: [0.017, -0.015, 0.012]
