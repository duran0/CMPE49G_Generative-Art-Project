# Cellular Bloom

Story:
This composition grows outward like a colony viewed under magnification. Individual centers remain visible, but the soft membranes between them create a larger organismic structure.

Method:
A set of random seed points generates both localized glow and a nearest-neighbor membrane field. After smoothing and adding low-frequency texture, banded contours transform the scalar field into clustered cellular forms.

Parameters:
- Seed: 48
- Resolution: 2000 x 2000
- Seed count: 48
- Sigma range: 0.08 to 0.15
- Membrane falloff factor: 18.0
- Texture smoothing sigma: 16.0
