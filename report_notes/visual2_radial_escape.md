# Radial Escape

Story:
This piece starts from a single origin and expands outward as a record of separation. Early motion remains concentrated at the center while later snapshots mark a widening field of possible paths.

Method:
Every particle starts at the same point and follows a pure diffusion process with no deterministic field. The visual overlays multiple time snapshots so color shifts outward with elapsed time.

Parameters:
- Seed: 42
- Particles: 1500
- Steps: 280
- Diffusion coefficient D: 0.0045
- Time step dt: 1.0
- Snapshot interval: every 4 steps
