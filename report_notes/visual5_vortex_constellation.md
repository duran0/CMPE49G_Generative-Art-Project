# Vortex Constellation

Story:
This piece imagines a sky governed by rotating wells rather than stars. Each particle is caught between competing circulations, so the image feels mapped and celestial while still remaining unstable.

Method:
Particles move through a vector field generated by four vortices with alternating rotation directions. A small diffusion term and a weak global confinement keep the motion lively while preventing the trails from leaving the frame.

Parameters:
- Seed: 77
- Particles: 350
- Steps: 600
- Diffusion coefficient D: 0.0028
- Time step dt: 1.0
- Core radius: 0.105
- Vortex strengths: [0.100, -0.099, 0.175, -0.105]
